What is dao?

The Taoist philosophy is a key component of traditional Chinese medicine and healing practices, and is based on the concept of Tao, which can be loosely translated as "the way" or "the path."


Taoism emphasizes the importance of balance and harmony in all aspects of life, including health and wellness. In the context of Chinese healing methods, Taoist principles are often used to guide diagnostic and treatment approaches.
